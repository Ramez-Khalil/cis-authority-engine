## Engagement Quality Scoring

Not all engagement is equal. Score each post's engagement quality on a 1-5 scale.

Score 5: Comments from C-suite, VPs, Directors, or senior IT professionals. Reposts by accounts with 1000+ followers. Comments that add substantive professional perspective.

Score 4: Comments from mid-level professionals. Reposts by relevant accounts. Comments that ask follow-up questions.

Score 3: Comments from students and early-career professionals. Comments that agree without adding substance. Profile clicks from relevant job titles.

Score 2: Generic positive comments. Likes without comments. Views from unverifiable audience.

Score 1: No engagement or irrelevant audience engagement.

Track quality score alongside quantity metrics to understand true content performance.
