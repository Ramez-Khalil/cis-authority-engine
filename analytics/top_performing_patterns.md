# Top Performing Patterns — CIS Authority Engine

## Purpose
This document is the analytical intelligence layer of the CIS Authority Engine. It is updated monthly based on engagement data from engagement_tracking.md and analysis of posts in posts/top_performing/. The insights here directly feed into prompt refinement and content strategy.

## How to Update This File
After 30 days of posting, analyze the top 20% of posts by engagement rate. Look for patterns across: hook category, framework type, controversy level, topic domain, audience targeting, line length and formatting, CTA structure, posting time, infographic use.

Document every pattern you find — even ones that contradict the initial strategy. Evidence overrides assumptions.

## Quarterly Strategy Review Checklist
Every 90 days, answer these questions using accumulated data:

1. What is the average engagement rate, and is it trending up or down?
2. Which three hook categories have the strongest consistent performance?
3. Which frameworks should be used more frequently, and which should be retired or revised?
4. What topics are generating the highest quality comments from target audiences?
5. Are there new CIS topics or industry developments that should enter the content rotation?
6. What changes should be made to the brand_voice.md, post_frameworks.md, or top_hooks.md files based on performance data?
7. Is the audience composition (executive vs student vs IT professional) shifting?
8. What is driving follower growth, and how can that pattern be amplified?
